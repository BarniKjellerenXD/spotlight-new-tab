## Short Description (132 chars max)
A beautiful spotlight-style search overlay for your new tab page. Search bookmarks, history, open tabs, and the web instantly.

## Full Description

**Spotlight New Tab** transforms your browser's new tab page into an elegant, keyboard-driven search experience — inspired by Zen Browser's spotlight and Vivaldi's Ctrl+E quick search.

### ✨ Features

- **🔍 Unified Search** — Type anything and instantly search across your bookmarks, browsing history, open tabs, and the web
- **⚡ Keyboard-First** — Navigate with `↑↓`, open with `↵`, open in new tab with `⌘↵`. No mouse needed
- **🏠 Quick Links** — Your most-visited sites appear as beautiful glassmorphism tiles when the search is empty
- **🎨 Glassmorphism Design** — Dark blurred backdrop, accent glow, smooth animations — matches modern desktop aesthetics
- **⚙️ Customizable** — Toggle search sources (bookmarks, history, tabs, web search), background blur, and time/date display
- **⌨️ Keyboard Shortcuts** — `⌘K` to focus search, `⌘B` for bookmarks, `⌘H` for history, `Esc` to clear
- **🔒 Privacy-First** — All data stays local. No analytics, no tracking, no data transmitted anywhere

### 🚀 How to use

1. Install the extension
2. Open a new tab — the spotlight search greets you
3. Start typing to search across your bookmarks, history, and open tabs
4. Press `↑↓` to navigate results, `↵` to open, `⌘↵` to open in a new tab
5. Press `Esc` to clear the search
6. Press `⌘K` anytime to refocus the search bar

### ℹ️ About Permissions

- **bookmarks** — To search your bookmarks
- **history** — To search your browsing history
- **tabs** — To find and switch to open tabs
- **topSites** — To show most-visited sites as quick links
- **storage** — To save your settings locally
- **search** — To trigger web searches via your default search engine

All processing is local. Nothing is sent to any server.
