## Full Description

**Spotlight** transforms your browser into an elegant, keyboard-driven search experience вҖ” inspired by Zen Browser's spotlight and Vivaldi's Ctrl+E quick search.

### Two ways to use it:
- **Press `Ctrl+E` / `Cmd+E`** from any page to open a floating spotlight popup
- **Open a new tab** for the full-page glassmorphism search with quick links

### вңЁ Features

- **рҹ”Қ Unified Search** вҖ” Type anything and instantly search across your bookmarks, browsing history, open tabs, and the web
- **вҡЎ Keyboard-First** вҖ” Navigate with `вҶ‘вҶ“`, open with `вҶө`, open in new tab with `вҢҳвҶө`. No mouse needed
- **рҹҸ  Quick Links** вҖ” Your most-visited sites appear as beautiful glassmorphism tiles when the search is empty
- **рҹҺЁ Glassmorphism Design** вҖ” Dark blurred backdrop, accent glow, smooth animations вҖ” matches modern desktop aesthetics
- **вҡҷпёҸ Customizable** вҖ” Toggle search sources (bookmarks, history, tabs, web search), background blur, and time/date display
- **вҢЁпёҸ Keyboard Shortcuts** вҖ” `вҢҳK` to focus search, `вҢҳB` for bookmarks, `вҢҳH` for history, `Esc` to clear
- **рҹ”’ Privacy-First** вҖ” All data stays local. No analytics, no tracking, no data transmitted anywhere

### рҹҡҖ How to use

1. Install the extension
2. Open a new tab вҖ” the spotlight search greets you
3. Start typing to search across your bookmarks, history, and open tabs
4. Press `вҶ‘вҶ“` to navigate results, `вҶө` to open, `вҢҳвҶө` to open in a new tab
5. Press `Esc` to clear the search
6. Press `вҢҳK` anytime to refocus the search bar

### в„№пёҸ About Permissions

- **bookmarks** вҖ” To search your bookmarks
- **history** вҖ” To search your browsing history
- **tabs** вҖ” To find and switch to open tabs
- **topSites** вҖ” To show most-visited sites as quick links
- **storage** вҖ” To save your settings locally
- **search** вҖ” To trigger web searches via your default search engine

All processing is local. Nothing is sent to any server.
